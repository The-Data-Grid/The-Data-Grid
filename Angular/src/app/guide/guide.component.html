> In order to optimize the accessibility of The Data Grid, we have developed a user guide to help one understand its capabilities and use it effectively.

Getting Started

The Data Grid provides centralization and management services for sustainability data such as electricity audits, water audits, and apiary inspections. Our public software suite creates specialized applications for your environmental project to allow for flexible data collection, upload, storage, and analysis.
